About Me

Background & Interests

I am Alisha Rath, currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science at San Jose State University, where I focus on advanced topics like machine learning, cloud computing, and backend development. I have a multi-disciplinary background, having worked extensively with technologies such as Java, Spring Boot, AWS, Python, and React.js. Before starting my master’s, I held a Lead Software Engineer position at Fidelity Investments, where I worked on streamlining UI interfaces and automating test suites, significantly improving the team’s efficiency and time to market. My experience also spans data science, machine learning, and blockchain projects, where I contributed to innovative solutions like converting transfer agent transactions to blockchain ledger format.

My personal interests revolve around problem-solving and competitive programming. I actively participate in coding challenges on platforms like LeetCode and HackerRank, where I’ve reached top levels. I enjoy keeping up with new technological advancements, particularly in artificial intelligence, cloud technologies, and software automation. I'm also passionate about creating open-source projects that make a positive impact and building systems that solve real-world problems.

Career Goals

My career goals include mastering advanced cloud services and AI systems while becoming a certified AWS Solutions Architect. I aim to take on leadership roles in large-scale software development projects, driving innovative solutions. Ultimately, I hope to work at the intersection of AI and cloud technology, developing intelligent systems that streamline processes and improve efficiency across industries. I’m particularly interested in using AI for legal decision-making systems and healthcare predictions, leveraging machine learning models to build robust solutions that can benefit society at large.
